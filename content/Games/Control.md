![[Control.png]]

From the creators of Alan Wake, this game has a great shot at being the most fun third person combat in any game ever. The telekinesis powers that you can use along with great shooting and movement make it so much fun to play. On top, it's mythos and environments are really original and well realized. An overlooked masterpiece.
