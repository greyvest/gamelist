Quirky and Janky in the way that Bethesda Games can only be, Fallout New Vegas realizes what has become an iconic setting in the bombed out Mojave. It's characters and storylines, and the RPG systems that lay on top of them, are expertly written and deeply interactive, and just a ton of fun to play with. It's some of the peak of RPG quest design.
