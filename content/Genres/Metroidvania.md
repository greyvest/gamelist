## Castlevania
The Castlevania games provide a super satisfying mythos around Dracula, Vampires, and the Belmont Family who have a generational quest to hunt them. At it's best with Symphony of the Night, the franchise makes backtracking and exploration fun and prioritizes gothic art. 
- [[Castlevania- Symphony of the Night]] is a must play, arguably the best in the genre. The castle in this game is so well realized, with it's twists and secrets, and it's music and gameplay leave nothing to be desired.
