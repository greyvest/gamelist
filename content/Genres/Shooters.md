## DOOM
It's not the first "Shooter" but it's the grandaddy of them all. This is one of the most important games ever, and you should definitely play some of it. 

## Quake
Where DOOM defined combat, movement, levels, and violence, Quake brought them into a much moodier space. Lighting and sound improvements made this game really special, and it's still an absolute blast to play. 

## Halo
Halo really brought shooters to the masses on the Xbox, and brought with it such stellar presentation and gameplay variety that it's still kind of the template for every shooter after it. 
You should play the trilogy, without question. Reach and ODST are also highly reccomended. 
Must Play : 
Halo 1
Halo 2
Halo 3
Should also play, because they're fantastic :
Halo Reach
Halo ODST
Okay Halo Games : 
Halo 4
Halo Infinite

## Counterstrike ( GO / 2 )
CS2 is a fantastic game, extremely grounded and very unforgiving. It's the sweatiest of the multiplayer shooters on this list, and is so simple as to have infinite depth in it's player expression. It's so competitive as to be very hard to enjoy in the short term, but provides a lot of satisfaction in getting better at it.
