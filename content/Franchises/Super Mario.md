It's hard to even begin to cover Mario and all it's iterations, but hopefully this can provide a shortlist of the most important games in the Franchise's run. 
What this really is, is a list of different genres that Nintendo has reached into and developed with their own special touch, and then put Mario on the front of it and found roaring success. Many of these games are made by different teams, but all of them are really great and often time genre-leading examples.

## 3D Platformers
Nintendo repeated their massive success from the 2D Mario games with their forray into 3D platformers. Super Mario 64 is a seminal game, and each game in the 3D platformer series after that improves and adds it's own unique and masterfully designed twists. Of all the series in games, this is one where you really cannot go wrong playing them all. 
- Mario 64 is such a solid, well designed, and revolutionary game as to still be vitally important to this day. 
- Super Mario Sunshine is the attitude era of early 2000's games on a beach, and adds a lot of really fun mechanics. Perhaps the least polished of the 3D platformers, but still extremely fun.
- Super Mario Galaxy adds so much variety and does so much innovative level design. A really spectacular game, and the sequel is considered just as good. 
- Super Mario Odyssey, the latest 3D Mario Platformer, continues a perfect streak of high quality games with massive variety in it's level design and mechanical interactions.

## Mariokart
The premiere Kart Racer, it needs very little introduction. A perfect party game.
- Mario Kart Double Dash is a super beloved early iteration that presents great multiplayer option where two players can team up on the same kart. 
- Mario Kart Wii followed double dash and had highlights of great tracks through the series, with really fun mini games.
- Mario Kart DS was a great portable version.
- Mario Kart 8 continued the tradition and is, by all means, maybe the best in terms of content and completeness. 
- Mario Kart World is a fun and interesting, though less fleshed out, iteration. It's the current Switch 2 option in stores and will have the most active online scene. 

## Party Games
Mario Party is a series of awesome mini games and a board game that will drive you to madness.
- Mario Party 2 or 3 for the N65 seem to be what the internet considers the best, but I really think the best Mario Party is whichever one you have around to play. 
- Mario Party DS is really great as well, and let you play with 4 friends when you only had one copy of the game. 
## RPG games
Mario RPGs have often been developed by Partner Studios like Square or Intelligent systems. This means that they've brought in a lot of talent from studios who are already working on deep RPGs, and who are applying Nintendo levels of polish to those games. What that results in is some really great stuff. 
- Paper Mario and the Thousand Year Door is an All Time Great, wonderful game. Funny, charming, beautifully rendered, suffeciently deep gameplay wise, and full to the brim of A+ game design. Don't miss this one. 
- Paper Mario, Super Mario RPG, and the Mario Superstar games are also widely beloved and made with a lot of heart. 

## Sports Games
Mario sports games are really good. They're fun, well designed, and very stylish. Utilizing the wide variety of Mario Characters for players in Soccer or Baseball provides a ton of fun variety to make great arcade sport experiences. 
Highlights : 
- Super Mario Strikers
- Super Mario Sluggers
- Mario Golf : Toadstool Tour

## Dr. Mario, PHD
Dr. Mario is it's own awesome little arcade game, definitely worth trying out. 

## Warioware
The Warioware games are similar to Mario Party in that they revolve around minigames, but do so in shorter bursts with higher variety. They're neat, quirky little games. 

## Luigi's Mansion
Really beloved games that take a very friendly Haunted Mansion aesthetic and apply some fun puzzle solving gameplay. The original Gamecube game is a classic.
